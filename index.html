About Us

We are the Cal Table Tennis Club, a student-run sports club in the University of California, Berkeley. We strive to provide a learning and casual environment for beginners as well as a competitive setting for advanced players. Students, faculty, alumni, as well as community members are welcomed to play with us.

Why Join

Ever wanted to beat that one kid in your dorm who always brags about being so good at ping pong but never could? Now you can! Joining Cal Table Tennis Club will give you the tips and much needed practice to do just that. Playing table tennis has many other benefits such as good exercise, meeting new people, and learning more about a really cool sport.

When and Where

We play at the RSF Fieldhouse with organized events every week usually on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays. There are also open recreation hours at the RSF, which can be found here:

Training

Every Friday from 4-7pm, we hold novice trainings for one hour time slots. On Sundays, there is intermediate training for the more advanced players who are looking to solidifying their skills. They are coached by our competitive team players, consisting of former national team members and even an olympian.
